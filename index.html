Overview


Pos: Average number of possession per team. Lower is slower. Possessions is the sum of ground controls, draw balls, and saves.
Pos %: Percent of a game possession's the team controlled. More is better.
O GPP: Number of goals scored per offensive possession.
Off Eff: Expect number of goals scored at a game with a Div 1 average number of possessions, 36 per team. Based on goals per possessions and % of possessions.
Opp Def Ave: Average defensive efficiency of opposition. Measures strength of schedule.
Off Eff Adj: Offensive efficiency adjusted for strength of schedule. Offensive efficiency multiplied by opponent defensive efficiency divided league average efficiency.
Def Eff: Expect number of goals scored on in a game with a Div 1 average number of possessions, 36 per team. Based on goals per possession given up and % of possessions that other team has the ball.
Opp Off Ave: Average offensive efficiency of opposition. Measures strength of schedule.
Def Eff Adj: Defensive efficiency adjusted for strength of schedule. Defensive efficiency multiplied by opponent offensive efficiency divided league average efficiency.
Eff: Efficiency is the sum of the offensive and defensive efficiency.
Eff Adj: Adjusted effency is the sum of the adjusted offensive and adjusted defensive efficiency.
